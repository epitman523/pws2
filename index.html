Bio

I am a graphic designer turned web developer based out of Southern Illinois near Saint Louis. While I enjoy designing, I’ve always felt a little too structured for the art world. For me, web development provides that perfect balance of logical thinking and artistic expression. Once I started teaching myself HTML and CSS, I knew I had found my calling.

My experience includes working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery and content management systems such as WordPress, Drupal and Adobe Experience Manager. To satisfy my inner-masochist I also enjoy building responsive emails.

When I’m not working you can find me kayaking, hiking, cooking or watching old MST3k episodes for the millionth time (not kidding, I’ve got most of them memorized).
